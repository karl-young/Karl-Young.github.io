Reflections

What are three new things you have learnt about yourself and your ego due to the core learning?

I have learned that I do have an ego, and it doesn't like to be embarrassed, which I am finding is affecting my level of confidence. This, in turn, affects my communication skills. I need to reach out and ask for help more often instead of just sitting there trying to figure it out by myself. I've also learned that this course has helped me understand my drawbacks and how to overcome them.

What are the roles of values, empathy, and self-awareness in learning and programming?

Values, empathy, and self-awareness play large roles in learning and programming, influencing not only how you approach technical challenges but also how you collaborate with others. Your values help you determine what is right and wrong when faced with complicated choices. Empathy is essential in working with teams, in client interactions, and in recognizing and addressing the diverse needs and backgrounds of users. Self-awareness can help you understand how you learn, what areas you can excel at, and which you may need improvement in. Understanding your communication style can also help you work with others.

What has surprised you the most about the core learning?

What suprised me, was how much help i needed and how difficult it was to ask for that help.

What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

Figuring out what to say and how to say it was a challenge for me when working on my Identity, values, and strengths blog. Some of those personality tests posed questions I couldn't answer definitively but still had to select an option. For example, in the Dope Bird Test, there was a question asking, 'Which one of these are you: Hotheaded, Vengeful, Rash, or Fearful?' I found it difficult to describe myself with any of those traits, and many of the tests had similar questions. As a result, I ended up writing a story about an experience that had happened to me that day in the hopes that it would convey my points effectively.

To touch the lives of people and encourage them to create through feelings of joy.

It doesn't feel like a waste of time. Yes, I could have used more time to practice programming, but the core part of this study will also help me work with other people.
